Penne Arrabbiata

ItemQuantity
Penne Pasta250 gms
Tomato4 medium
Tomato Paste2 tbsp
Garlic5 cloves
Olive Oil2 tbsp
Grates Parmesan Cheese1/4 cup
Basil Leaves5 nos
Red Pepper Flakesas required
Salt/ Pepperas per taste
  1. In a pot of heavily salted boiling water, cook the pasta till al dente.
  2. Drain the pasta and cool it with running water.
  3. Blanch the tomatoes, remove their peels and blend them.
  4. Heat oil in pan, add in chopped garlic, blended tomatoes, the tomato paste and cook them on low for 30 minutes.
  5. Add in the cooked pasta and some pasta water and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Garnish it with cheese and basil and optionally serve with your choice of grilled veggies.
