Chicken Pan Pizza

ItemQuantity
Precooked Pizza Crust2 nos
Tomato3 medium
Tomato Paste2 tbsp
Onion3 medium
Garlic5 cloves
Chicken Breast1 nos
Oil2 tbsp
Shredded Mozzarella Cheese1 cup
Butteras required
Red Pepper Flakesas required
Dried Oreganoas required
Salt/ Pepperas per taste
  1. To prepare the marinara sauce, first blanch the tomatoes, remove their peels and blend them to a paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add in 2 finely chopped onions and cook them on medium heat till it sweats.
  3. Add in diced garlic, red pepper flakes and saut√© for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add in blended tomatoes and the tomato paste and cook on low heat till it thickens.
  5. Season it with salt, pepper and dried oregano as per taste. The sauce for pizza is ready.
  6. Heat some oil in a pan and add diced chicken and cook on medium heat.
  7. When it is almost done, add in a diced onion and any vegetable topping of your choice.
  8. Cook them on the highest setting so they develop a char. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. In a thick pan, add some butter and place the pizza crust on it.
  10. Spread our prepared sauce and add in the toppings and cheese.
  11. Close the lid and let it bake for 5-10 minutes till the cheese melts.
