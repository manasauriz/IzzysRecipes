Hakka Noodles

ItemQuantity
Noodles250 gms
Carrot1 medium
Capsicum1 medium
Onion1 medium
Garlic3 cloves
Ginger1 inch
Green Chillies1 nos
Scallions1 cup
Oil2 tbsp
Soy Sauce1 tsp
White Vinegar1 tsp
Toasted Sesame Oil1 tsp
Salt/ Pepperas per taste
  1. Julienne carrot, capsicum and onion and finely chop scallions, ginger, garlic and green chillies.
  2. Heat oil in a wok/ pan and saut√© ginger, garlic and green chillies for a minute.
  3. Add in the sliced veggies and cook them on high heat for 5 minutes, tossing regularly.
  4. Boil 4 cups of water and salt in a pot and cook the noodles for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Drain the noodles and add them with the vegetables.
  6. Add in soy sauce, vinegar and sesame oil to the noodles and season it with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with scallions and optionally serve it with boiled/fried eggs.
