Chicken Noodle Soup

ItemQuantity
Chicken Breast1 nos
Noodles250 gms
Carrot2 medium
Celery1 medium
Onion1 medium
Garlic3 cloves
Chicken/ Vegetable Stock2 cups
Oil3 tbsp
Cilantro1 cup
Lemon Juice1 tsp
Salt/ Pepperas per taste
  1. In a thick-bottomed pan, heat some oil and sauté chopped garlic for a minute or two.
  2. Dice the chicken into cubes and add it to a pan and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  3. Add in roughly chopped carrots, celery and onion to the chicken and sauté them for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add in the stock, water as required and add in uncooked noodles.
  5. Simmer the soup till the noodles and chicken are cooked properly.
  6. Garnish it with chopped cilantro, lemon juice and season it with salt and pepper.
